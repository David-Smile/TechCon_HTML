Our History

Founded in 2015, TechCon began as a small gathering of technology enthusiasts in Silicon Valley. What started as a 200-person event has grown into one of the world's premier technology conferences, attracting over 10,000 attendees annually. Our journey reflects the rapid evolution of technology itself, from the early days of cloud computing to today's AI and quantum computing revolutions.

Our Mission

"To inspire innovation, foster collaboration, and drive technological advancement through meaningful connections and knowledge sharing."

At TechCon, we believe in the power of technology to transform lives and businesses. Our mission is to:

  • Bridge the gap between emerging technologies and practical applications
  • Create a platform for thought leaders to share groundbreaking ideas
  • Foster a community of innovators and problem-solvers
  • Promote diversity and inclusion in the technology sector
